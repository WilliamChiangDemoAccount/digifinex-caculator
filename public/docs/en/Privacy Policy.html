Ⅶ. How do we protect your Personal Information

  1. We have implemented industry-standard, reasonably practicable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, public disclosure, use, modification, damage or loss of data. We will take all reasonably practicable steps to protect your Personal Information. We implement physical, technical and administrative security measures to reduce the risk of loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure and alteration, including, but not limited to, transport layer data encryption, firewalls and encrypted storage, physical access controls, and information access authorization controls. For example, we ensure that all your network communications with us are protected by encryption using encryption technology (SSL). Your Personal Information is encrypted using high strength encryption and stored on our servers. We use trusted protection mechanisms to protect Personal Information from malicious attacks. We use a variety of data desensitization techniques, including content substitution and SHA256, to enhance the security of Personal Information during use, such as Personal Information display and Personal Information association calculations. We have an industry-leading data security management system that focuses on the core of data and revolves around the data lifecycle, enhancing the security of the entire Website in multiple dimensions from organization construction, system design, personnel management, and product technology to protect your Personal Information.
  3. We will take all reasonable and feasible measures to avoid collecting irrelevant Personal Information, and we will only retain your Personal Information for as long as necessary to achieve the purposes described in this Policy, unless the retention period needs to be extended or is permitted by law. After the necessary period, we will delete your Personal Information, except as otherwise required by law or regulation. When our products or services are discontinued, we will notify you by push notification, announcement, etc.
  4. Please be aware and understand that the Internet is not a completely secure environment, and you should take measures to protect your Personal Information. We have set up a transparent data breach handling system, and in the unfortunate event of a Personal Information security incident, we will inform you of the basic circumstances and possible effects of the security incident, the measures we have taken or will take to deal with it, suggestions for your own prevention and risk reduction, and remedial measures for you, as required by law and regulation.
